SUNDAY's HOUSE

Sunday's House is a thoughtfully designed single-story, five-bedroom residence crafted to meet the evolving needs of a nuclear family while allowing for seamless expansion to accommodate extended family living. Spanning 255 square meters (20.45m by 18m), the home balances functionality and spatial fluidity, ensuring comfort, privacy, and adaptability. The ground floor is designed as the heart of daily living, featuring two en-suite bedrooms, a mini lounge for intimate gatherings, a spacious main living area, a well-appointed kitchen with a pantry, a dedicated dining space, and a utility room with an integrated staircase. The first floor expands on this layout with three additional en-suite bedrooms, a cozy family lounge, a private office, and an open sit-out terrace—creating a harmonious blend of shared and personal spaces. Situated on a generous 1,536-square-meter site, the property extends beyond the main house with a detached two-bedroom staff quarters, a gatehouse, and landscaped outdoor areas that enhance the living experience. From the earliest design stages, expansion has been a key consideration, with provisions made for future extensions. The client’s ongoing pursuit of additional land underscores the vision of transforming the property into a more expansive family estate.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Conceptual and schematic design,
  • Detailed architectural design and drafting(AutoCAD).
  • Construction documentation and site supervision.
  • Project management and coordination.

Sunday’s House exemplifies a forward-thinking residential design approach blending flexibility, comfort, and long-term planning to support both present and future living needs.
