UHL EVENT CENTER

UHL Event Center is a 3,995-square-meter multi-functional facility designed to revitalize a previously passive urban district, transforming it into a dynamic hub for events, social gatherings, and recreation. The architectural vision prioritizes adaptability, ensuring the center can accommodate a wide range of events while integrating seamlessly with its urban context. At the heart of the design is a spacious 50.4m by 33.85m event hall, capable of hosting 480 guests at 60 round tables. A secondary 300-seat hall provides additional versatility, with a modular layout that allows for reconfiguration into a larger auditorium when needed. Complementing these indoor spaces is a 389.9-square-meter outdoor event area, offering an open-air setting for social functions. Other amenities include a conference room, a mini eatery, a swimming pool with adjacent bar and grill, and ample parking facilities—all carefully arranged to enhance user experience and movement flow. Designed with flexibility in mind, the center features adjustable floor levels near the stage, enabling customized setups for different event formats. The landscape design incorporates sustainable elements that contribute to the aesthetic appeal and ecological resilience of the space, reinforcing its role as a community landmark.

UHL Event Center is more than a venue—it is an adaptive, sustainable, and community-oriented space that fosters engagement and elevates urban experiences.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Drafting, Preparation of construction documentation and 3D modeling with Autodesk Revit
