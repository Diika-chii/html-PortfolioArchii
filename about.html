About Me

As an architect with over a decade of experience, I specialize in creating thoughtful, user-centered designs that prioritize both individual and collective needs. My expertise lies in designing family homes that balance diverse preferences, aspirations, and budgets, while fostering spaces that enhance everyday living. I approach each project as an opportunity to deeply understand my clients' world immersing myself in their aspirations, interests, and challenges to deliver tailored solutions. My passion for cultural ideas in architecture led me to pursue a second master's degree in Architecture and Cultural Heritage in Dessau, Germany. This experience deepened my understanding of how collective memory, social dynamics, and shared histories influence user decisions and preferences. It has refined my ability to design spaces that resonate emotionally, drawing from past experiences while anticipating future needs.

In addition to my architectural expertise, I am skilled in AutoCAD, Revit, ARCHICAD, Lumion, MS Office, and MS Project, with project management experience in Agile methodologies and Scrum Master skills. I am also proficient in CSS, HTML, JavaScript, and UX design fundamentals, enabling me to integrate digital interfaces and interdisciplinary user experiences into my work. Beyond my professional pursuits, I am inspired by travel and photography, which provide fresh perspectives and creative insights. Comparing the dynamic contrasts between Africa and Europe has enriched my design philosophy and broadened my understanding of cultural narratives. In my downtime, I enjoy social settings, whether playing a game of billiards or chess, or simply engaging in lively conversations. Ultimately, my goal is to design adaptable, timeless spaces that not only meet current needs but also inspire and become a cherished heritage for future generations.
